Content Rewriter v1

Prompt Engineering • Copy Cleanup • Deliverables

Summary

Converted a cluttered property description into clear, buyer-friendly copy. Produced two variants and headline options using a structured prompt (role, goal, context, constraints).

Role: Senior copy editor
Goal: Improve clarity & conversion
Constraints: 5th–8th grade, no jargon, 2 variants + headlines

Before / After

Before

Excerpt of original text:

Appraisal ended up hurting us the most... (full text in downloadable file)

After — Version A (120–180 words)

We listed our 2,400 sq. ft. landscaped home... (concise, readable, value-forward)

After — Version B (80–120 words)

We listed our upgraded Texas home... (short punchy)

Download client-safe deliverable

Process

  • Gather raw input and define audience + objective.
  • Apply universal rewrite prompt with constraints.
  • Generate two variants and 3 headlines.
  • Document results and package client-safe version.

Outcome

Delivered clean, market-ready copy emphasizing location advantages and upgrades. Simple framework that generalizes to marketing, support, and ops content.
